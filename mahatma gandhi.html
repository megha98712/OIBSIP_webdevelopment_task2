Bhagat Singh

1907 – 1931
“They may kill me, but they cannot kill my ideas. They can crush my body, but they will not be able to crush my spirit.”

Biography

Bhagat Singh was a revolutionary freedom fighter who played a pivotal role in India’s struggle for independence. He is remembered for his courage, patriotism, and willingness to sacrifice his life for the nation.

He became a symbol of resistance against British colonial rule and inspired generations with his fearless ideology.
