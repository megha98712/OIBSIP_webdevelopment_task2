Biography

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, popularly known as the "Missile Man of India", was an eminent scientist and the 11th President of India.

He made significant contributions to ISRO and DRDO, leading India's missile and nuclear programs.

He inspired millions with his words, books, and vision for India 2020.

He passed away on 27 July 2015 while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong.
